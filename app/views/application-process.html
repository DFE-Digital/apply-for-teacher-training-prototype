How the application process works

Most teacher training courses are one year and start in September when the academic year in England starts. Part time courses are usually 2 years.

The application process for courses starting in September 2023

You can start applying for courses a year before they start. For courses starting in September 2023, you can start applying in October 2022.

Date and time What happens
4 October 2022 at 9am You can start to find postgraduate teacher training courses you might want to apply to.
11 October 2022 at 9am You can submit your application to training providers.

You can apply to 4 courses and each course will be treated as a different application.

You should make sure each course you choose is to a different training provider so you can have a better chance of success.

Training providers will have 40 working days to decide to offer you a place on a course you applied to. If they do not respond in time, your application will become unsuccessful.

If all your applications become unsuccessful, you can apply to any courses again.

You will have 20 working days to respond to any offers you receive. If you do not respond, the offer will be automatically declined.
9 December 2022 to
6 January 2023		 This period is not included in the ‘40 working days’ that training providers have to make a decision on your applications.
27 March 2023 to
10 April 2023		 This period is not included in the ‘40 working days’ that training providers have to make a decision on your applications.
1 July 2023 The time training providers have to make a decision about your application is reduced from 40 working days to 20 working days.
5 September 2023 at 6pm If you have not already applied between October 2022 and this date, you will not be able to apply after 5 September 2023.

If you've already applied, but your applications were unsuccessful, you can continue to submit new applications.
19 September 2023 at 6pm This is the last day you can apply for courses starting in September 2023.
27 September 2023 at 11:59pm Training providers need to make a decision on all applications for courses starting in September 2023.

Continuous application process

How the application process works

Most teacher training courses are one year and start in September when the academic year in England starts. Part time courses are usually 2 years.

The application process for courses starting in September 2024

You can start applying for courses a year before they start. For courses starting in September 2023, you can start applying in October 2023.

Date and time What happens
3 October 2023 at 9am You can start to find postgraduate teacher training courses you might want to apply to.
10 October 2023 at 9am You can start submitting your applications to training providers.

You can submit up to 4 applications at a time. You should make sure each course you choose is to a different training provider so you can have a better chance of success.

You can withdraw an application at any time and apply to a different training provider if you want to.

Training providers will have 40 working days to decide to offer you a place on a course. If they do not respond in time, you'll be able to submit another application while you wait for a response. We'll tell you if the provider has not responded in time.

We'll tell you when you receive an offer from a training provider, so you can respond.
18 December 2022 to
5 January 2024		 This period is not included in the ‘40 working days’ that training providers have to make a decision on your applications.
17 March 2024 to
1 April 2024		 This period is not included in the ‘40 working days’ that training providers have to make a decision on your applications.
1 July 2024 The time training providers have to make a decision about your application is reduced from 40 working days to 20 working days.
3 September 2024 at 6pm If you have not already applied for teacher training, you will not be able to apply after 3 September 2024.

If you've already applied, but your applications were unsuccessful, you can continue to submit new applications.
17 September 2024 at 6pm This is the last day you can apply for courses starting in September 2024.
25 September 2024 at 11:59pm Training providers need to make a decision on all applications for courses starting in September 2023.
