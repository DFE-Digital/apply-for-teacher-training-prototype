Apply for teacher training

Use this service to apply for a postgraduate teacher training course to teach in a state primary or secondary school, or in further education.

This service is for courses in England. Learn more about teacher training in Wales, Scotland and Northern Ireland.

You can save your application and come back to it at any time.

How the teacher training application process works for courses starting in September 2024

Most teacher training courses are one year and start in September when the academic year in England starts. Part time courses are usually 2 years.

You can start applying for courses a year before they start. For courses starting in September 2024, you can start applying in October 2023.


Date and time What happens
3 October 2023 at 9am You can start to find postgraduate teacher training courses you might want to apply to.
10 October 2023 at 9am You can start submitting your applications to training providers.

You can submit up to 4 applications at a time. You should make sure each course you choose is to a different training provider so you can have a better chance of success.

You can withdraw an application at any time and apply to a different training provider if you want to.

Training providers will have 40 working days to decide to offer you a place on a course. If they do not respond in time, you'll be able to submit another application while you wait for a response. We'll tell you if the provider has not responded in time.

We'll tell you when you receive an offer from a training provider, so you can respond.

1 July 2024 The time training providers have to make a decision about your application is reduced from 40 working days to 20 working days.
3 September 2024 at 6pm If you have not already applied for teacher training, you will not be able to apply after 3 September 2024.

If you've already applied, but your applications were unsuccessful, you can continue to submit new applications.
17 September 2024 at 6pm This is the last day you can apply for courses starting in September 2024.
25 September 2024 at 11:59pm Training providers need to make a decision on all applications for courses starting in September 2023.

Get advice and support

You can register with Get Into Teaching, the Department for Education’s free support and advice service.

A specialist adviser will help you prepare your application and advise you on funding options (including loans and tax-free bursaries and scholarships). You can also book school experience and access teaching events.
